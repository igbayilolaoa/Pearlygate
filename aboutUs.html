Our Story
We are a real estate company with a zeal to be the best, full service Real Estate company in the state to enhance the quality of life of the people through our active community involvement.
What sets PREL apart from the rest is the availability of vibrant and hands on team of experienced professionals in key positions to ensure our clients get optimum satisfactions every time they invest and transact with us.
Unlike other Real Estate companies, Pearlygate Real Estate Limited incorporates proven, professional state of the art techniques, specializing in the management, acquisition, marketing, listing and selling of new homes, resale of luxury homes, residential communities, condominiums, homes and undeveloped lands.
We launched Pearlygate to leave every client that patronize us with a lasting impression that for us, our dignity, honesty and prestige is important.
Our Mission
Delivering home ownership everywhere by helping our clients build generational wealth through real estate investments such as valuation, facility management, property management, construction supervision, mortgage process, property documentation and surveying also helping them create and maintain optimum return and total satisfaction from their investments through reliable guidance and advice on acquisition, sales and management, leaving them with a positive lasting impact.
Our Vision
We envision being the #1 choice when it comes to providing real estate products and services, doing all we can in a distinct way to help our clients achieve their investment aspirations in order to get optimum returns
